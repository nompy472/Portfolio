Welcome to School of Chords

Instruments and Lessons

We teach Guitar, Ukelele, Piano, Recorder and Flute in Pretoria and Johannesburg. We offer lessons to learners from ages 5 to 18.

We operate as an extra mural activity at several Primary Schools, High Schools, Nurseries Schools and Aftercare Centers in Pretoria and Johannesburg.

We also offer a "Taught at home" programme where we send an instructor to teach you at the comfort of your own home.
